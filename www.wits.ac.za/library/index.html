Architecture Library Together with the Engineering Library, the Architecture and Built Environment Library serves primarily the Faculty of Engineering and the Built Environment.
Biophy Library Biophy is the scientific library and covers the following schools: Animal, Plant & Environmental Sciences, Chemistry, Molecular & Cell Biology, and Physics.
Commerce Library Serves schools within the Faculty of Commerce, Law, and Management. as well as access and study needs of students registered with Wits Plus.
Education Library The Education Library supports the activities of the Wits School of Education and therefore maintains a strong print collection in the field of education.
Engineering Library Together with the Architecture Library, the Engineering Library serves primarily the Faculty of Engineering and the Built Environment.
GeoMaths Library Serving the school's of Computer Science and Applied Mathematics, Geography, Archaeology and Environmental Studies, Geosciences, Mathematics and Statistics, and Actuarial Sciences.
Historical Papers Archive Historical Papers is one of the largest and most comprehensive independent archives in Southern Africa housing over 3400 collections.
Law Library The Law Library is located in the Oliver Schreiner building and serves the information needs of the School of Law.
Health Sciences Library The Witwatersrand Health Sciences Library was established in 1923 as the Witwatersrand Medical Library, to support the medical practitioners on the Witwatersrand.
William Cullen Library This library named after Chemist and Metallurgist, William Cullen, is home to many special collections, government publications, and the Africana Collection.
Wartenweiler Library The Wartenweiler Library is a multifaceted service point, availing three floors of library books, as well as other important and essential services.
Library of Management The Wits Library of Management serves primarily the needs of the Wits Business School and the Wits School of Governance in collaboration with the Commerce Library.
